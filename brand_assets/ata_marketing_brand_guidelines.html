Logo Clearspace
Ata Marketing
Minimum clearspace = half the height of the logo mark on all sides. Never crowd the logo.
Minimum Size
→ 20px min height
→ Digital preferred
Do not use the logo below 20px height in digital. For print, minimum 8mm.
Usage Rules
✓ Do
Use the logo on brand-approved backgrounds (dark, cream, gold).

Keep the wordmark and mark together at all times.

Use gold as an accent color, not a dominant background.

Maintain generous whitespace around all brand elements.
✗ Don't
Stretch, rotate, or distort the logo in any way.

Place the logo on busy or clashing backgrounds.

Use unauthorized typefaces alongside the logo.

Recreate the logo in different colors outside the palette.
